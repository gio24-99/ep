ERP system- Enterprise resource planning (ERP) is a software system that helps 
you run your entire business, supporting automation and processes in finance, human resources, 
manufacturing, supply chain, services, procurement, and more.
Advantages of ERP - Precise planning - We can calculate extremely precise costs of new business ideas, with much less probabilty of failure. 
                    Easy access to management systems - We can see every department status in live.For example we always now how many ceratin products are left in warehouse.
                    Higher management perfomance - It reduces needs of paperwork and manual data input, so there is less chance to make a human based error.
                    Faster coordination - ERP system has minimal communication delay between departments of the company. Especially if company has network in multiple states or countries.
Disadvantages of ERP - Large cost of ERP system development - Enterprise resource planning requires experienced team with years of experience. Good skills will ask for higher wages, so custom made ERP system will be pricey.
                       However there are premade ERP systems on the market, which are lower in price, but for ceratin companies it will be less flexible, resulting in poor perfomance.
                       Maintnance cost - After deploying ERP system it is not finished like that, company needs IT staff, because ERP system often need updates or even upgrades to make it more flexible for customer needs.
		       So maintnance also ends up in big expenses, however after certain time, these expenses will be payd off.
		       Training,Practice and Adaptation - Deploying ERP is not enough, It takes time for staff to learn at least basics of ERP and then master its full potential. Also not all staff members will be welcome for
                       this system, because It will be hard for them, or they would think that they will lose their job because of it. That is because they are scared of new systems, because of it very often company has to fire 
	               employees that behave like that.
	               Takes time to utilize - ERP development is long and complex process, with stages of deployment, old data input, customization and etc which sometimes result in delays.
